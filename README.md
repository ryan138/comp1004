Smart Assignment Planner
Overview
The Smart Assignment Planner is an offline web app designed to help university students manage their coursework and deadlines. 
It runs entirely in your browser without needing an internet connection or extra downloads.  
FeaturesTrack Assignments: Add, edit, and delete your tasks easily.  
Stay Organised: Sort assignments by deadline or priority, and filter them by specific modules.  
Update Statuses: Quickly mark tasks as "To Do", "In Progress", or "Done".  Add Files: Attach small PDFs or images (up to 300 KB) directly to your tasks.  
Export Data: Download your assignment list as a JSON file to keep a backup.  
Private & Secure: Your data is encrypted and saved directly on your device, locked behind a passphrase you choose so it stays private even on shared computers.  
How to Use
Download the smart_planner_fixed.html file.Double-click the file to open it in any modern web browser .  
When prompted, enter a secure passphrase. This acts as your password to securely lock and unlock your data. 
Start adding and managing your assignments!
Built With
Plain HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.  
No external frameworks or servers required.
